Myth of the Stars: Zodiacs

Capricorn

           Capricorn was a goat mountain spirit who had helped the gods. In exchange for his help, the gods put him in the sky.


Aquarius

           Aquarius was a Trojan prince that Zeus brought to Olympus to be a cup bearer to the gods. He was later put into the sky and renamed Aquarius.

Pisces

           Pisces were two fish that have a few different stories. In one story they helped Aphrodite to shore after she was born from sea foam. In another myth they helped Aphrodite and Eros escape from a monster. In both cases, Aphrodite wanted them in the sky for their help.


Aries

           Aries, the ram, had golden fleece. Its golden fleece was part of the quest that Jason and the Argonauts sought after.


Taurus

Gemini

           Gemini is based on the twins, Castor and Pollux, from the Jason and the Argonauts story.


Cancer

           Cancer, the crab, was a monster that went to the aid of the hydra when it was fighting Hercules. After the it died, Hera decided to put him into the sky as a constellation.


Leo

           Leo is the Nemean Lion, one of the monsters killed by Hercules during his twelve labors. After the lion died, Hera decided to put him into the sky as a constellation.


Virgo

           Virgo is Astraea, the goddess of justice. Zeus decided that she would become the constellation Virgo.


Libra

           Libra is the scales that Astraea uses to judge people.


Scorpius

           One of the beasts that Orion hunted. In a version of the Orion myth, it was Scorpius that killed Orion.


Sagittarius

           Sagittarius, also known as Chiron, was a centaur. He trained many heroes such as Jason and Achilles.
